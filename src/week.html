TYPICAL WEEK

Here's what a week in the life of a Computer Scientists life at the Students' Union might look like.

MONDAY

The start of the week is the best time to stock up on supplies. Going to the shop and buying all your food and any stationary you might need is a great idea!

TUESDAY

Tuesdays are heavy days for Computer Science Students, 9 till 6. So why not recharge in the middle of the day at Coffee Revolution and drive yourself to the end of the day.

WEDNESDAY

With all that free time on Wednesdays, why not make use of it in our study area? Or even better, go buy tickets at the box office to the various types of coding workshops offered by the university's societies during the week to fill up your free time!.

THURSDAY

Prepare for Friday night by buying a ticket to poptarts or any other of our venues!

Friday

The week is over, the stress can subside for a couple days and you're ready to relax. Celebrate reaching Friday by grabbing a pint at Interval and wait for it all to blow over.
