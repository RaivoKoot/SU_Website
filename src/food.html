FOOD AND DRINK

A university runs on its students, who run on their stomachs. So we have the widest variety of options for food here at our Students' Union, take a look!

Coffee Revolution

Programmers are a machine that converts caffein and sugar into lines of code. Why not fuel yourself up with cake and coffee in Coffee Revolution? With a relaxed atmosphere and comfy seating, you'll feel right at home.

Bubble Tea

Weird the first time, but you'll grow to love it. Boba tea with tapioca pearls is a must try experience! Stop by your bubble tea shop to try their range of drinks.

Interval Cafe

The Students' Union's hidden gym. Nestled under the building, a friendly warm cafe and bar. One of the best places to hang out after a long week. With both food and drinks available here, there will be something to your liking.

Proper Pasty Company

There's nothing quite as comforting as a warm pasty. Hot, filling , delicious and with a piping hot cup of tea to wash it down. Meat and vegetarian options to boot!

Garden View Deli

Burritoes, burgers and big savings for a poor student. The view deli provides a meal and view. Look over the landscape of Sheffield while enjoying a hot meal.

Food Court

The biggest and most extensive eating area within our Students' Union. Uni Central has many outlets for even more types of food. So you'll never be lost for what to eat. And even better, after dark, it becomes one of the best places to study and spread out all your notes on the tables and work in peace.
