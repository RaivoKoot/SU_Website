FACILITIES

If you need advice on your course, a place to buy some environmentally friendly food, or simply a place to sit and study; our Student's Union has it all.

SERVICES

SSID

Meet SSID, the Student Service Information Desk. Your local encyclopedia of advice! If you need any advice or infomation on University, social life or personal queries. SSID can help!

Job Shop

For that extra bit of cash, why not enquire in our job shop? They can give you advice and access to jobs inside and outside the university. With experienced, knowledgeable staff and A vast array of resources, the Job Shop will find you a job which you'll love.

Front Desk

Quite literally the face of the Students' Union. Our representative will always be happy to help, give advice and tell you where to go to find out more infomation.

SHOPS

Students' Union Shop

Our very own shop! Our stock ranging from international food, to paper clips, to seasonal decorations, we have you covered! Student needs at student prices, what more do you need

Zero Waste Shop

Save the planet while saving your money. The Zero Waste Shop allows you to buy goods for as little waste, and as little environmental impact, as possible. If you need oats or oil, or otherwise, the Zero Waste Shop has it.

Box Office

The box office is quite literally your ticket to uni life! After a hard week of study, buy a ticket to poptarts and wind down and relieve all the stress built in the week. Our own box office sells tickets to parties we put on in the foundry or fusion rooms. And it also sells tickets to films in our own cinema! Be sure to stop by!
